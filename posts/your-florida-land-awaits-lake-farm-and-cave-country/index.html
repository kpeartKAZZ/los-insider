The one Hubert keeps circling back to —

Full Acre in Marianna — Just over an acre on a quiet residential street, minutes from downtown Marianna and its county hospital — the kind of steady town that keeps a rental full. If you want a lakeside lot renters pay a premium for —

Lakeside Lot on Round Lake — A third-acre on the edge of a quiet 30-acre freshwater lake, ten minutes from Marianna. Lake access is the kind of line that lets a rental command more every month. Lower entry than the Marianna acre. $499 down, $339/mo.

1.1 Acres Near the Suwannee River, Jennings — The closest of the four to Albany, with Valdosta just 25 miles north. Site-built homes are welcome, the Suwannee River is minutes away for fishing and camping, and it's running 25% off the cash price right now. $499 down, $401/mo.

And if you want the lowest price and the strongest return —

Quarter Acre in Dunnellon — Paved roads, power at the street, and R1 zoning that keeps out mobile homes and protects your rental comps. Lowest cash price of the four at $18,997, minutes from the crystal-clear Rainbow River. $299 down, $301/mo.
