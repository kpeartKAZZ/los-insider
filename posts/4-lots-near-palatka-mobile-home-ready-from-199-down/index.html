4 lots near Palatka ☀️ mobile home ready from $199 down

Tyrone called me from Jacksonville last week looking for land where he could put his double-wide. He wanted the Interlachen and Palatka area — no HOA, owner financing, and room to breathe. We found four lots that fit everything he asked for.

If what Tyrone was looking for sounds like what you want too, keep reading. All four are zoned for manufactured and mobile homes. All four come with owner financing starting at $199 down. No credit check.

If you want to farm it, grow it, and live free —

Half an Acre on E Alachua Road, Palatka — Nearly half an acre of AG-Agricultural land outside Palatka. Farming, animals, and your double-wide are all welcome here — agricultural zoning means no gray area. Power access already at the street from neighboring properties. Downtown Palatka is just down the road when you need it, but your land sits quiet and separate. $399 down, $340/mo.

If you want maximum flexibility and the lowest possible entry —

0.77 Acres on Manchester Ave, Interlachen — The largest lot on this street. I walked this one myself. Three-quarters of an acre of wooded forest, four minutes from FL-315 and FL-20. R2-zoned, manufactured home ready. Room to set up your place and still have forest privacy on every side. $299 down, $341/mo.

Manchester Ave, Interlachen — 0.77 acres, wooded, R2-zoned. $299 down, $341/mo.

0.38 Acres, Interlachen — Nearly four-tenths of an acre in the wooded heart of Interlachen. The zoning literally says "R2 (Mobile Home Allowed)" — zero ambiguity. No HOA. Twenty minutes to downtown Palatka. $249 down, $330/mo.

0.35 Acres on Cockscrow Road, Satsuma — A third of an acre near Palatka, R-2 zoned for manufactured homes. The most budget-friendly monthly of the four. Dirt road access, power nearby. A solid choice if you want to keep your costs low while you get set up. $199 down, $319/mo.
