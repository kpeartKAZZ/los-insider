Land Deals ☀️ $299 Down Near Florida Springs and Lakes

Published by Lots of Sunshine · FL Florida Land Arkansas Land Owner Financing Land Investing Market Updates

Nyshiem called me last week about a full acre near Marianna, and his plan stuck with me: build a home he can live in, add a second unit, and let the rent help carry the note. Live in one side, rent the other — on his own ground, near water that runs clear all year.

So this week I pulled the same three lots I sent Nyshiem — all owner financed, all near clear spring water, all with room to build. Down payments start at $299.

If you want room to build — and room to grow —

Cave Country Acre, Marianna — Just over a full acre on a quiet residential street, right in the middle of Florida's cave country. Room for a home now and a second unit later — or split it into two half-acre lots down the road. Jackson Blue Springs runs clear and 68 degrees year-round a few minutes up the road, and the walk-through caverns are practically neighbors. $749 down, $348/mo.

A full acre near Marianna — room to build, room to grow, minutes from the springs.

If you want to wake up to lake water —

Lakeside Living on Round Lake — Step out your front door and see water. This lot sits on the edge of a quiet 30-acre freshwater lake, about ten minutes from downtown Marianna, in the same springs-and-caverns country. Build the way you want — no bank telling you what kind of home is allowed. $499 down, $339/mo.

If you want the springs, the simple way in —

Rainbow Springs Starter, Dunnellon — A quarter acre minutes from Rainbow Springs, some of the clearest water in the state, made for swimming and tubing. Paved roads, power at the street, no HOA. High and dry and ready to build, with room to park an RV while you do. The lowest-commitment way onto land near the springs. $299 down, $329/mo.

Minutes from Rainbow Springs in Dunnellon — paved roads, power at the street, and room to build for $299 down.

Here is the part that makes the plan work: every one of these lots is owner financed at zero percent interest, so every payment goes straight to principal. No credit check, no prepayment penalty. You put a little down, build when you are ready, and the land does the waiting for you.
