Own Your Piece ☀️ 12 New Florida Lots From $199 Down

Published by Lots of Sunshine · FL Florida Land Arkansas Land Owner Financing Land Investing Market Updates

Today I'm dropping 12 new lots from the Panhandle to Central Florida. Down payments start at $199. Every single one is owner financed — no bank, no credit check, no gatekeepers.

If you want to live on the water —

Lakeside Lot, Round Lake — A quiet 30-acre freshwater lake right at the edge of your door. Any home type welcome: site-built, manufactured, mobile, or tiny. This is the kind of place people stop driving around and start putting down roots. $499 down, $399/mo.

Camellia Drive, Indian Lake Estates — This community wraps around Polk County's largest lake — 7,500 acres, nationally known for bass fishing. The neighborhood has a marina, an 18-hole golf course, and the longest freshwater pier in the state. 1.1 acres with paved roads and power already in. $749 down, $346/mo.

Deep River Court, Placid Lakes — Placid Lakes sits on the shore of Lake June-in-Winter. Paved roads, a community boat ramp, and neighbors who've been here for decades. Quarter acre, zoned for a permanent site-built home. $199 down, $248/mo.

Sunway Drive, Placid Lakes — Same community, same lake access on Lake June-in-Winter, same boat ramp. A second lot a few blocks away if Deep River Court is already spoken for. $199 down, $248/mo.

Round Lake, Northwest Florida — 30-acre freshwater lake, any home type. $499 down, $399/mo.

And one near a wonder of the natural world —

Lilac Court, Marianna — Florida Caverns State Park — the only walk-through cave system in the state — is under five miles away. Jackson Blue Springs pumps 64 million gallons of crystal-clear 68-degree water a day, year-round. This is a full acre in a quiet residential neighborhood right in the middle of all of it. Any home type allowed. $749 down, $253/mo.

If you want to farm it, grow it, and live free —

Lisa Ann Trail, Palatka — Agricultural zoning on 1.25 acres means you can grow crops, raise animals, and build the home you want — all on the same piece of land. That combination is rare. Road access is in. Palatka and the St. Johns River are 10 minutes away. $499 down, $359/mo.

If you want to hunt, camp, and hold something that grows in value —

NW 27th Way, Jennings — 2.6 acres on the Suwannee River Corridor, right off I-75 at the Georgia line. Big Shoals State Park — the state's only Class III whitewater — is 20 minutes south. Wide open land where the pace is slow and the price still makes sense. $1,199 down, $398/mo.

James Street, Flagler Estates — 1.25 forested acres in a county where the land across the road sells for three times more. Road access in. Hunters and patient investors both win here. St. Augustine is 35 minutes northeast. $199 down, $249/mo.

Dona Street, Flagler Estates — Second lot in the same community, same acreage, same price point. The window to buy at today's prices in Flagler Estates won't stay open forever. Palm Coast is 25 minutes east when you need it. $199 down, $249/mo.
