If you want land that earns its keep —

R-2 Land in Satsuma — 0.35 acres and the largest of the three, zoned Two-Family so a duplex is yours to build from day one. Quiet dirt road, power nearby, twenty minutes from downtown Palatka. $199 down, $319/mo.

Satsuma — a quiet street, power at the road, and room to build two doors instead of one.

Wooded Lot in Interlachen — 0.38 acres and the most flexible of the three. Modular, manufactured, even a mobile home are all welcome here. A wooded slice of quiet where you build it your way, no one telling you no. $249 down, $330/mo.

Build-Ready Lot in Satsuma — 0.32 acres, also Two-Family zoned, and the lowest cash price of the bunch at $17,997. Same Satsuma stretch as the first one, so you could walk both in a single visit. $199 down, $259/mo.

Interlachen — wooded, undeveloped, and open to almost any kind of home you want to put on it.
