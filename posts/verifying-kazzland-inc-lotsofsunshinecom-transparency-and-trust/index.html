🌿 Verifying Kazzland, Inc. / LotsofSunshine.com — Transparency and Trust

Published by Lots of Sunshine · AZ Florida Land Arizona Land Land Investing Market Updates

Hello,

Thank you for reaching out and for taking the time to verify who you’re working with — we completely understand the importance of feeling confident and secure before moving forward with a land purchase.

Transparency and trust are top priorities for us at Kazzland, Inc. and LotsofSunshine.com.

Below are a few simple, direct ways to confirm our company’s legitimacy and ownership of properties we sell:

1. County Verification (Property Ownership)

Each property we sell includes the Assessor’s Parcel Number (APN). You can verify ownership directly through the county’s official website for that parcel — you’ll see Kazzland, Inc. listed as the deed holder.
(For example: Apache County, AZ – https://www.apachecountyaz.gov)

2. Business Registration
Our company is officially registered and active in the State of Florida. You can verify this anytime on the Florida Division of Corporations (Sunbiz) website:
🔗http://www.sunbiz.org

Simply search for Kazzland, Inc. to view our registration details and status.

3. Better Business Bureau (BBB)
You can also check our standing and reviews with the Better Business Bureau (BBB):
🔗 https://www.bbb.org

Search “Kazzland, Inc.” or “LotsofSunshine.com.”

4. Social Channels & Client Testimonials
We encourage you to explore our active online presence — we regularly post educational content, real client testimonials, and behind-the-scenes updates about our work and properties.
