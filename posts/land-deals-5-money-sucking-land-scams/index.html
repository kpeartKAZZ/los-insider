Published by Lots of Sunshine · MULTI Owner Financing Land Investing Market Updates

At LotsofSunshine.com, we buy tons of properties a year.

And just like you, we see listings that look perfect—great photos, great price, even a great story behind them. But are they real?


That’s why I created this breakdown: the 5 biggest land scams you need to watch out for.

Scam Stories You’ll Learn About

  1. Brent the Bad Broker – He sold the same property twice, before the county records caught up.

  2. Nathan the Naïve Nephew – Thought his dead uncle’s will gave him the land. Spoiler: it didn’t.

  3. Fractional Francine – Only owned half the property, but tried to sell the whole thing.

  4. The Taxation Disaster – One missed tax bill, and the county sold the land out from under us.

  5. The Zoning Fiasco – Looked buildable, but was actually inside a conservation overlay zone.

Each of these stories comes with simple solutions you can use today. Think of it as your “deepfake detector” for land deals.
